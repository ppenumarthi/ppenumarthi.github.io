Phani Krishna Penumarthi

About Me

I am Phani Krishna, a final year PhD candidate in Computer Science Department at University of South Carolina.
I am advised by Prof. Srihari Nelakuditi for my explorations in Computer Networking area.

I am now in the job market, looking for an industry position.

Research Interests

I am interested in Designing, Building and Evaluating several protocols in SDN/LTE Networks, emphasizing security and reliability of the packets.
Previously, I earned my Master of Science by Research degree from CSE department of Indian Institute of Technology Madras.
