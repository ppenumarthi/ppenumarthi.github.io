Phani Krishna Penumarthi

About Me

I am currently working as a software engineer at PaloAlto Networks.
Previously, I earned my PhD in Computer Networks from Computer Science Department at University of South Carolina, 2020.
Prior to that, I also earned Master's degree specialized in WiMAX Networks from Computer Science Department at Indian Institute of Technology Madras, 2012.

Research Interests

I am interested in Designing, Building and Evaluating Networking protocols in SDN/5G/Classical Networks, emphasizing on: Eliminating packet drops in the network.
